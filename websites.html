Noble Crow Tournaments

Noble Crow Tournaments was a website I built to help run Warhammer Miniatures Game tournaments at local game stores. When the Pandemic hit we stopped doing the tournaments and to save money I took the website offline.

Operation Abolitionist

boe-our.net is a website I built to help organize, sell tickets and track tickets for the Operation: Abolitionist charity events I ran with my Airsoft Team. We initially ran the event with Black Angels Recon, the Airsoft team I started and later with Black Ops Elite, a well established team in the community.

DemiGoddess

DemiGoddess is a website I created for my Mother's small business where she makes skin care products using her bio-chemistry degree and background in cosmetics and kelates.

Arcane Odyssey

Arcane Odyssey is a Dungeons and Dragons style Role-playing Game that my older brother and I have been creating. We have kept the same base rules of 5th edition but created the entire world including the majority of races, classes, spells and abilities. It takes place in a universe where we assume the 'aliens' on other planets are elves, dwarves, orcs and more! With Magic and Technology combined, the possibilities are endless!
